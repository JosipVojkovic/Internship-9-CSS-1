Will L.A.’s Fires Permanently Disperse the Multigenerational Black Families of Altadena?

The Benn family left the Jim Crow South and found affordable housing and a deep sense of community in Altadena. After the L.A. fires, all but three of their extended family’s houses are gone and they don’t know how or if they’ll be able to stay together. Emily Witt reports on the aftermath of the Eaton Fire.
